About

I am currently a freshman at NJIT majoring in Human-Computer Interactions. I love this major because it combines computer science, design, and psychology, three fields I love and enjoy doing. I hope to obtain a position as a UX designer, for it is a direct way to see how my projects impact and benefit others in day-to-day living.

I have always loved designing and creating, having extensive experience with Adobe Suite and digital and traditional art. I also enjoy programming, where I see my code become alive as a program. Languages I know include Python, HTML, and CSS.

I am currently interning as a technology assistant at a non-profit organization. Where I do graphic design and CAD, and I am also assisting with the creation of a coloring app. I have some UX experiences in the field in internships and projects I have done. And I would love to build on top of that and gain more valuable experiences.

In my free time, I also like to play video games, and I am in the process of creating my own game. I also enjoy drawing and music. I have done yearbook club throughout high school and newspaper club in college, where I can continue to utilize my graphic design skills. I also enjoy teaching, I have been teaching and tutoring since high school, and I am continuing it by teaching kids how to code.
